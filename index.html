Goal:

Our mission is to make it easier and more focused for students to view the courses they have chosen at Illinois Tech. We want to help as many students as possible take the courses they prefer within the limited add/drop time periods, and we want students to have a basic understanding of their instructors before they enroll.

Application Features:

Students can write reviews or study tips(but we'll check their reviews first then decided posted on the app or not) for classes they have already taken to help future students who choose that course, and to help students prepare for classes they will take.
